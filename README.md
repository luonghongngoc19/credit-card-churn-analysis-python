# Credit Card Customer Churn Analysis | Python

## Overview

This project analyzes credit card customer data to identify key factors related to customer churn and provide actionable business insights to improve customer retention.

Dataset: Credit Card Customers (Kaggle)

Total records: 10,127 customers

Churn rate: ~16%

---

## Tools Used

- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## Business Objectives

- Identify customer segments with high churn risk
- Analyze behavioral and demographic factors driving churn
- Provide recommendations to improve customer retention

---

## Key Analysis Performed

### Customer Demographics

- Age distribution
- Gender analysis
- Income category analysis

### Customer Behavior

- Transaction amount analysis
- Transaction count analysis
- Credit limit utilization

### Relationship Analysis

- Products owned vs churn rate
- Customer tenure vs churn

---

## Key Insights

- Overall churn rate is approximately 16%
- Female customers have higher churn rate than male customers
- Customers with low and very high income have higher churn risk
- Customers using fewer banking products have significantly higher churn
- Customer engagement is strongly related to retention

---

## Business Recommendations

- Focus retention strategies on high-risk segments
- Increase cross-selling to improve customer engagement
- Develop churn prediction models for early intervention
